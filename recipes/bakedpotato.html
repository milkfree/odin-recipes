Baked potato

Juicy potato cunty way to spend your dinner time, follow up the recipe and enjoy the perfection of culinary

What you will need

  • Potato (duah)
  • Olive oil
  • Peper & Salt
  • Mayo

Asssuming you have oven, water, pod, time, apartment, hands, life, gravity etc.

  1. Buy or harvest the potato
  2. Boil them (potato) in boiling hot water for 1h
  3. Put them (potatos) in some container what can handle 250c influence
  4. Season it with winter, summer and a little bit of autumn
  5. Add Paper, salt, and mayo
  6. Shove it to the heated 200c oven for 1h
  7. Spin the wheel to 250c and leave it(potato) for another 30 min
  8. profit
